# Acknowledgements

WorldCovers builds on the work of generations of philatelists who have documented the postal markings of the United States.

## Chip Gliedman

Special thanks to **Chip Gliedman**, who initiated the digitization effort, designing and implementing the first version of the software from which WorldCovers is built. 

## The U.S. Philatelic Classics Society

The American Postal Markings Catalog is a project of the U.S. Philatelic Classics Society, who entrusted WorldCovers with delivering it.
