# Frequently Asked Questions

## Do I need an account to read documentation?
No. The Help area, including this FAQ and the glossary, is available to guests without signing in.

## What can I do as a guest?
Guests can browse the catalog and documentation pages. Actions that modify data, such as submitting contributions, require authentication.

## Where can I find definitions for system terms?
Open the Glossary article in Help. It explains commonly used terms such as cover, marking, contribution, and citation.

## How do I submit a correction or new record?
Sign in and use the contribution workflow to propose your change. Contributions are reviewed before being published.

## Why is my contribution not visible yet?
New submissions usually require moderation. Check your dashboard or submissions area for current review status.

## What should I include in a high-quality contribution?
Provide clear structured details, include supporting references when possible, and attach evidence that helps moderators validate the proposal.

## Can I edit or delete an existing catalog entry directly?
Most users cannot directly edit live records. Instead, submit a contribution or update request that goes through moderation.

## How do I report incorrect documentation?
Use your team’s normal feedback channel and include the document title and the specific section that appears outdated or unclear.
